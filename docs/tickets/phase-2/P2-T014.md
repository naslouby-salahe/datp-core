**Idempotent-rerun finding.** `LocalThresholdStrategy` (B2) and `FamilyThresholdStrategy` (B3) were already fully implemented in `infrastructure/thresholding/policies.py` and integration-tested (`tests/integration/thresholding/test_exact_adapters.py::test_shared_local_and_family_strategies_use_their_locked_identity_and_scores`) from an earlier undocumented commit. **Verified acceptance criteria.**
- B2 local p95: `LocalThresholdStrategy.assign()` returns `local_value if eligible else fallback` per client — existing test confirms `(1.0, 3.0, 5.0)` (each client's own threshold) for a 3-client fixture with all clients eligible.
- B3 unweighted family mean: `_family_value()` computes `unweighted_shared_threshold` over eligible family members only — existing test confirms `(2.0, 2.0, 5.0)` (clients 0/1 share a family, mean of 1.0/3.0; client 2 is its own family).
- B3 blocked without eligible taxonomy members: **new integration test** `test_b3_family_construction_rejects_a_family_with_no_eligible_members` proves `FamilyThresholdStrategy` raises `ThresholdError` when a client's resolved family contains no eligible member (via a purpose-built `_OtherOnlyFamilyMemberships` double whose returned family excludes the querying client itself, since the existing fixture's family helper always includes the querying client). This is the closest concrete, testable manifestation of "B3 requires taxonomy" at the strategy layer: true taxonomy *authorization* (matching a caller's declared `family_taxonomy_id` against a registered experiment profile) is generic Phase-1-owned config-matching machinery (`config/mapping/scientific.py::_matches_threshold_configuration`'s `FamilyThresholdConfig` case), unchanged by and out of scope for this ticket, which owns `infrastructure/thresholding/policies.py` construction only.

- Config resolution: B2's `percentile` and B3's `percentile`/`family_taxonomy_id` resolve from `configs/scientific/thresholds.yaml`'s `quantile_grid` (percentile) and the experiment-level `family_taxonomy_id` field (already present in `config/schemas/scientific.py::FamilyThresholdConfig`, pre-existing Phase 1 work, unchanged here) — both code-level formula invariants (B2 local-p95, B3 unweighted-family-mean) per this ticket's own "Required configuration" clause.
