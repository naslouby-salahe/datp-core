14. **Why this ticket exists.** Architecture §9.1 states that fit rows are the authorized TRAIN rows in `SplitManifest` only, and that `PreprocessingSpec` owns preprocessing chunk sizing exclusively — never duplicated in `ResourceBudget`, `TrainingSpec`, or `ScoreGenerationSpec`. Architecture §29.4 lists "fitting preprocessing on TEST" as an invalid state made unrepresentable; §16.3 requires a two-pass fit where exact TRAIN-fitted statistics require it, enforced by `NormalizationScope`. If `FittedPreprocessorResult` could carry a TEST-derived statistic, or if chunk sizing were duplicated across specs, a normalization statistic computed even partially from TEST rows would silently leak evaluation-set information into every downstream score, defeating the calibration/evaluation separation the whole pipeline depends on.
