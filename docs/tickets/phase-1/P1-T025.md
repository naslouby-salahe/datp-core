14. **Why this ticket exists.** Architecture §9.2 fixes B4's fingerprint order and forbids `q` from ever entering it, because a `q`-mutated fingerprint would let cluster membership silently drift across percentile cells and defeat the "assignment reused unchanged across q" guarantee; Architecture §16.2 further establishes that a fixed seed alone does not guarantee stable clustering across scikit-learn releases, so canonical B4 must pin the exact `KMeans` algorithm, `n_init`, `max_iter`, and scaler scope rather than leaving them as caller-tunable knobs. Roadmap §17 and SB-26 lock `B-FedStatsBenign`'s pooled-variance formula to include the between-client mean-shift term before any computation, and SB-27/SB-32 forbid a fixed-k primary comparator and a post-hoc K choice respectively — this ticket is where those roadmap locks become non-bypassable types rather than configuration options.
