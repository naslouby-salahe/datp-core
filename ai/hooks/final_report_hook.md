# Final Report Hook

## Trigger
Before final response.

## Purpose
Verify completion is reported with evidence.

## Blocking status
Blocks final response.

## Required checks
- Contract completion verified.
- Changed files listed with reasons.
- Tests and checks listed accurately.
- Cleanup result stated.
- Remaining risks and skipped checks reported.

## Failure behavior
Do not finalize until the report includes the required facts and no claimed check is fabricated.
