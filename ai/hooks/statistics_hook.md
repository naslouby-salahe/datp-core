# Statistics Hook

## Trigger
After result, metric, statistics, seed, or interpretation changes.

## Purpose
Protect statistical validity.

## Blocking status
Blocks completion.

## Required checks
- Metric direction, seed pairing, BCa CI, Wilcoxon, Cliff's delta, sign consistency, q-sensitivity, and weak/null interpretation checked where relevant.

## Failure behavior
Fix the statistical issue or report the blocker and affected evidence.
