# Comment Hook

## Trigger
After edits to code, docs, configs, examples, or prose.

## Purpose
Keep comments and docstrings accurate and useful.

## Blocking status
Blocks completion.

## Required checks
- No stale comments.
- No stale docstrings.
- No AI-generated comments.
- No banner comments.
- No decorative comments.
- No misleading or bloated explanations.

## Failure behavior
Remove or correct bad comments in touched scope.
