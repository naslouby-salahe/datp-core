# Claim Evidence Map

## Purpose
Map every claim to evidence.

## When to apply
Apply to manuscripts, README claims, result summaries, captions, diagrams, and reviewer responses.

## Blocking rules
Block unsupported claims, overgeneralization, causal wording without causal evidence, and claims not tied to a table, figure, result artifact, config, protocol rule, or literature source.

## Pass criteria
Each claim has evidence and a classification: confirmatory, supportive, mechanism, stress test, boundary, exploratory, or future work.

## Fail criteria
Evidence is missing, claim strength exceeds evidence, or stress-test evidence is used as confirmatory proof.
