# Statistical Validity Check

## Purpose
Validate statistical design and interpretation.

## When to apply
Apply to result code, tables, figures, metrics, summaries, and claims.

## Blocking rules
Block incorrect metric direction, broken paired seeds, BCa CI misuse, Wilcoxon misuse, Cliff's delta misuse, sign inconsistency, q-sensitivity errors, and unsupported claims.

## Pass criteria
Metrics, seed pairing, intervals, tests, effect sizes, signs, and wording match the protocol.

## Fail criteria
Weak or null results are exaggerated, metric direction is wrong, or confirmatory evidence is misclassified.
