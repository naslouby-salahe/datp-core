# Literature Overlap Check

## Purpose
Check novelty and citation honesty.

## When to apply
Apply to related work, novelty language, abstract claims, discussion, reviewer responses, and manuscript framing.

## Blocking rules
Block unverified first claims, ignored thresholding or federated-threshold overlap, and novelty wording that hides personalization, conformal, quantile, or Laridi-style risks.

## Pass criteria
Novelty language is specific, bounded, and supported by relevant literature.

## Fail criteria
The text claims novelty without verification or treats related work as irrelevant without evidence.
