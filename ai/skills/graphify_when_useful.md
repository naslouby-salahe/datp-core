# Graphify When Useful

## Purpose
Use diagrams to reduce text and clarify relationships.

## When to apply
Apply to dense workflows, dependency logic, experiment matrices, and claim hierarchies.

## Blocking rules
Block decorative diagrams, diagrams that imply unsupported claims, diagrams that change DATP meaning, and diagrams that increase clutter.

## Pass criteria
The diagram replaces or compresses text and preserves evidence boundaries.

## Fail criteria
The graph is ornamental, misleading, or less clear than concise prose.
