# Reviewer Attack Check

## Purpose
Surface likely harsh-reviewer objections.

## When to apply
Apply before finalizing claims, result interpretations, manuscript sections, or submission-facing summaries.

## Blocking rules
Block unresolved high-risk objections about B2 tautology, 9 devices, Laridi overlap, personalization absorption, weak external validation, and overclaiming.

## Pass criteria
Objections have severity, evidence status, exact fix, and safe wording.

## Fail criteria
The work hides reviewer risks or treats missing evidence as resolved.
