# Comment Docstring Hygiene Check

## Purpose
Keep comments and docstrings accurate and useful.

## When to apply
Apply after edits to code, docs, configs, examples, manuscripts, or governance files.

## Blocking rules
Block stale comments, stale docstrings, AI-generated comments, banner comments, decorative comments, misleading comments, bloated explanations, and compatibility comments that preserve stale behavior.

## Pass criteria
Comments explain non-obvious intent and match current behavior.

## Fail criteria
Comments repeat obvious code, preserve stale history, or look generated.
