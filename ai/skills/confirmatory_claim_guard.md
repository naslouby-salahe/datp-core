# Confirmatory Claim Guard

## Purpose
Protect the sole confirmatory endpoint.

## When to apply
Apply to claims, result summaries, abstracts, captions, discussions, and tables.

## Blocking rules
Block confirmatory claims outside Regime A, B1 vs B2, CV(FPR), 10 paired seeds, BCa CI, and positive delta excluding zero.

## Pass criteria
Confirmatory, supportive, mechanism, stress-test, boundary, exploratory, and future-work claims are labeled accurately.

## Fail criteria
Supportive or exploratory evidence is written as confirmatory, causal, robust, deployment-ready, privacy-preserving, or first without proof.
